# Milamula Project Instructions

Milamula is an exploratory children’s story, education, and activity product line.

## Strategic Direction

- Validate one paid kit before building a subscription service.
- Keep the MVP content-first and parent-facing.
- Do not add subscription billing, accounts, child profiles, LMS features, gamification, marketplace features, AI generation, or automated fulfillment without explicit approval.
- Prefer simple static/content-first pages unless backend capture is intentionally added.

## Product Offer

The first offer is `Milamula Adventure Kit #1`:

- short story
- printable activity pages
- parent guide
- coloring/activity page
- simple learning objective
- optional parent-supervised 3D-printed character/object bundle
- preorder or interest capture

Recommended product tiers:

- PDF-only activity kit
- printed worksheet pack
- printed worksheet pack plus 3D-printed bundle

## Child Safety And Claims

- Do not claim products are baby-safe, toddler-safe, food-safe, or medically/therapeutically beneficial unless verified.
- Treat 3D-printed items as parent-supervised activity objects.
- Avoid tiny parts, sharp edges, fragile pieces, and choking-risk designs.
- Include supervision language wherever 3D-printed objects or physical activities are mentioned.
- Avoid medical, developmental, diagnostic, or therapeutic claims.

## Design Direction

Milamula should feel warm, imaginative, trustworthy, parent-friendly, educational but not academic, cute but not cheap, and suitable for parent-child activity time.

Use mobile-first layouts, soft visuals, clean spacing, editorial storytelling, and clear CTAs.

## Data Capture

If backend capture is added, collect:

- parent name
- phone or WhatsApp
- optional email
- child age range
- preferred kit format
- preferred theme
- willingness to preorder
- notes
- consent to be contacted

Until backend capture is connected, clearly label forms as interest/preorder placeholders.
